Frequently Asked Questions

What age group can be a part of this hackathon?

This hackathon has been made by students and for students! So any student from 6th to college who has a strong passion for coding can be a part of this wonderful event.

What do we need to get?

The participants are requested to get their own laptops, keyboards, mouses and other electronic accessories required. Internet connection will be provided at the venue.

When and where is the event?

The event will be held at Harvest International School Sarjapur on the 27th and 28th of January 2024 (tentatively) from 8:30am to 5:30pm on BOTH DAYS. Details will be shared on our website and social media pages after registration.

I am a beginner. Can I still participate?

Absolutely! Whether you're a tech enthusiast, a problem solver, or just curious about the world of technology, this event has something for everyone!

What is the maximum number of participants in a team? Can I go solo?

The minimum number of participants in a team is 2 and the maximum is 4. If you don't find any teammates, please feel free to contact us at hi@synthax.tech. We will ensure you get a fellow teammate

I want to be a sponsor. Where do I contact?

Sure! If you are interested in sponsoring the Synt-haX hackathon 2024, you may contact us through mail - hi@synthax.tech
